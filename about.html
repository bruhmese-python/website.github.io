About Us

The Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Cell

IEDC SNGCE began 20 onwards. IEDC SNGCE is funded by KSUM. Our aim is to inculcate Innovation, Creative thinking skills of students with the help of events that extend in developing socially uplifting projects and to bring those ideas into reality. Our propaganda with the help of trainings, workshops and competitions help build an environment for the betterment of skills of individuals. Planning and training of the students are overlooked upon by the faculty members who play a vital role in the endeavours. Proudly being a part of owning one of the Mini FabLabs in the State, we aim to inculcate skills with minimal training to develop their own technology for personal or commercial use. OUR VISION To create an innovation culture among Innovators by introducing them the State-of-the-art technologies and positioning the Institution as a Learning and Innovation Platform by delivering technically competent and skilled Entrepreneurs. OUR MISSION To create IEDC as an Innovation Platform and to create future founders by promoting Innovation, Technology and Business Learning among student community IEDC IN SNGCE Our Objectives : To promote an innovation driven entrepreneurship culture among the students To develop and promote commercially viable innovative product and solution from the students. To promote enterprise among budding technopreneurs and thereby creating more employment opportunities To bridge the gap between Industry and Academia
